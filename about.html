A LUXURY SALON EXPERIENCE IN THE HEART OF THE FINANCIAL DISTRICT NYC







KAEZ SUPER SPA IS THE PERFECT PLACE TO GET AN EXCEPTIONAL HAIRSTYLE FROM TOP-NOTCH, INTERNATIONALLY TRAINED STYLISTS AND COLORISTS.

Our bright and well-designed Downtown salons, complete with relaxing shampoo massage chairs, are a great inspiration of purity and modernity.
